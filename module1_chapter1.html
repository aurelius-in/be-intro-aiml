Chapter 1: What is AI?

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of study that aims to create machines capable of intelligent behavior. It involves the development of algorithms that allow computers to learn from and make decisions based on data.

Key Concepts

AI encompasses a wide range of technologies and methodologies, including machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics. AI systems can be trained to perform tasks such as recognizing patterns, making predictions, and automating processes.

Conclusion

Understanding AI is crucial as it continues to impact various industries and aspects of everyday life. This chapter provides a foundational overview of AI concepts and their applications.
