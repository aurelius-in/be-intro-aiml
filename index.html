Course Overview

This course introduces you to the basics of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). You will learn about fundamental concepts, important terminology, and various applications of AI in different industries.

Module 1: Basics of AI and ML

In this module, we will cover the basic principles of AI and Machine Learning, including their definitions, types, and key differences.

Module 2: Fundamental Concepts and Terminology

This module focuses on fundamental concepts and important terminology in AI and ML, such as algorithms, models, training data, and more.

Module 3: Overview of AI Applications

Here, you will explore various applications of AI across different industries, highlighting real-world use cases and the impact of AI on society.

Module 4: History of AI

This module delves into the history of AI, tracing its development from early concepts to modern advancements, and discussing significant milestones in the field.

Module 5: Key AI Technologies

We will explore key technologies that drive AI, including neural networks, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics.

Module 6: AI in Different Industries

This module examines how AI is transforming various industries such as healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and transportation.

Module 7: Ethical Considerations in AI

Finally, we will discuss the ethical considerations and challenges associated with AI, including bias, privacy, and the societal impact of AI technologies.
